NanoPad

Made with love and no AI by Centipede5

This project was conceived as a simple site to solve all sorts of text routing issues on the internet. Ive found most solutions either clunky (sending stuff to yourself over messaging apps), filled with unnessisary friction (like pastebin or github), or missing key features like truly secure notes.

Here's a run-down of what makes this truly different:

  1. Absolutely free
  2. Decentralized using Gun.js
  3. Data encrypted using YOUR password-based keys - there is 0 way for anyone without this key to see your data (me included)
  4. Open-source
  5. Trivially self-hosted (just fork the repo! no server needed!)
